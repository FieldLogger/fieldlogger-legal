Privacy Policy for FieldLogger

Last Updated: November 27, 2025
Effective Date: November 27, 2025

Introduction

FieldLogger ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the FieldLogger mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Authentication Information

  • Apple ID: We use Sign in with Apple for authentication. We receive a unique user identifier and, if you choose to share it, your email address.
  • Name: If provided through Sign in with Apple, we store your name for personalization.

Task and Content Data

  • Tasks: Text and metadata about tasks you create (title, creation date, completion status, tags).
  • Voice Recordings: Audio recordings you create are temporarily stored locally for transcription processing.
  • Transcriptions: Text transcriptions of your voice recordings generated by OpenAI's Whisper API.

Device Information

  • Device ID: We collect a device identifier for crash reporting and analytics.
  • Usage Analytics: We collect anonymous analytics about app usage (number of tasks created, features used) via Firebase Analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Service: Enable task creation, voice recording, and transcription features.
  • Sync Your Data: Store your tasks in CloudKit for multi-device sync (when enabled).
  • Process Voice Input: Send audio recordings to OpenAI or Anthropic APIs for transcription and AI-powered task parsing.
  • Improve the App: Analyze anonymous usage patterns to improve features and fix bugs.
  • Communicate: Send important service updates (we will never send marketing emails without consent).

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Tasks and user data are stored locally on your device using Core Data.
  • Voice recordings are temporarily stored during processing and deleted after transcription.

Cloud Storage

  • When multi-device sync is enabled, tasks are stored in Apple's CloudKit.
  • CloudKit data is encrypted in transit and at rest using Apple's security standards.
  • We do not have direct access to your CloudKit data.

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI: Voice recordings are sent to OpenAI's Whisper API for transcription. OpenAI does not store audio data after processing. OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic: Task text may be sent to Anthropic's Claude API for parsing. Anthropic does not train on user data. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage data is collected via Firebase. Firebase Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly approve data sharing.
  • Service Providers: OpenAI, Anthropic, and Firebase process data on our behalf under strict privacy agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: View all tasks and data stored in the app.
  • Delete Your Data: Use the "Delete Account" feature in Settings to permanently delete all your data.
  • Export Your Data: Request a copy of your data by contacting us.
  • Revoke Access: Revoke FieldLogger's access via Settings → Apple ID → Sign in with Apple at any time.

Children's Privacy

FieldLogger is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, all data is permanently deleted within 7 days.
  • Voice Recordings: Deleted immediately after transcription (typically within 60 seconds).

International Users

FieldLogger is available globally. By using the app, you consent to data processing in the United States, where our servers and third-party services are located.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance.
